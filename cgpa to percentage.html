CGPA stands for "Cumulative Grade Point Average". It is measure used in education to evaluate a student's overall academic performance over a certain period, usually an entire course or program. Now while filling the application forms of various companies, many times we need to fill the percentage achieved in the course because there are many companies who accept percentage rather than CGPA. Everyone does not know the process of converting CGPA to percentage. This CGPA to percentage calculator will deffinitely help to easily calculate the percentage of the respective CGPA.
